Who is Filip

From designing on plates, to designing on screens. I’ve gone from being a head chef to a designer and though my platform has changed, my mission remains the same: to create an exceptional experience.

My first though about user experience emerged about 15 years ago when I eat my first meal with chopsticks. That day I’ve realized that to create an unforgettable experience for my customers I can’t thing only about the ingredients and the recipe, but also about the utensils, the color of linens, lighting & décor of a dining room…
